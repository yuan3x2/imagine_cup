About

Our target and main ideas

Individuals with hearing impairments often opt for text or sign language for communication, limiting their social engagement. Relying on sign language interpreters for real-time translation is hindered by limited availability and the need for advanced reservations, creating challenges in various situations.

We propose a unique real-time sign language translation device that instantly converts spoken language into sign language. This direct interpretation approach is more efficient for individuals with hearing impairments, setting our device apart from existing solutions.

Our translation device is ideal for large events, ensuring hearing-impaired individuals can fully participate in public speeches where sign language interpreters may be lacking. Its implementation not only enhances inclusion but also streamlines resource use by overcoming the limitations of interpreter availability.
